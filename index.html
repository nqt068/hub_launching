Mission

Hub-Launching Strives to Empower, Elevate, and Inspire Youth Entrepreneurship in Hanoi through Unity, Creativity, and Collaborative Innovation.

01 Empowering

We aspire to be the leading force in Hanoi, dedicated to empowering and nurturing the next generation of entrepreneurs. Our comprehensive suite of services and resources is meticulously crafted to support and elevate youth-led startups, fostering an environment where innovative ideas flourish into successful ventures.

02 Elevating

Grounded in creativity and collaboration, we actively contribute to the evolution of Hanoi's startup ecosystem. Our initiatives extend beyond individual hubs, creating an interconnected network that propels collective success. Through strategic alliances and innovative approaches, we aim to elevate the overall environment for entrepreneurial growth and prosperity.

03 Inspiring

Guided by the commitment to creativity and cooperation, we endeavor to inspire and shape entrepreneurial mindsets among the youth. Our mission is to foster a deep understanding and appreciation for entrepreneurship, providing platforms for effective communication, educational programs, and collaborative learning initiatives. By equipping the youth with the skills and mindset needed to navigate the dynamic startup landscape, we aim to create a generation of well-rounded and innovative entrepreneurs.
